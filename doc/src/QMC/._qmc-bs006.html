For discussion

  • Is this of interest to us as a lab?
  • There is a lot of theory activity with promise of circumventing some of the traditional theory bottlenecks (increased dimensionalities etc etc).
  • Quantum computing (QC) and quantum information (QI), and Machine Learning, are topics we believe could be of great interest to the lab, experiment and theory

The faculty recommends that the laboratory provide matching funds for the MSU NSF Quantum Leap Challenge Institute proposal, with matching costs proportionally shared by the participating MSU departments and units according to the amount of NSF funds awarded. The establishment of an MSU Institute for Quantum Science and Computing would provide new opportunities for laboratory research and workforce development.
